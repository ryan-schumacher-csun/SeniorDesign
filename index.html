Ram-Yum is a mom and pop style ramen shop that is not afraid to do things its own way in order to provide a delicious meal. From generation to generation our kitchen will become like family to you. We strive to make you not only feel comfortable, but to enjoy your time at our shop.

Why Choose Ram-Yum Ramen?

Our goal is to make everyone who orders our food and steps into our business to feel like home and have a comfortable place to stay and enjoy their meal. Ram-Yum is more than just tasty ramen, but a new take on a classic bowl of noodles and broth.
